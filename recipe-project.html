Recipe project

It's the end of May 2020 and I've been on this coding journey for around 6 months (somewhere in the region of 100 hours of study and practice). It's time to build something from scratch and discover things organically, alongside syllabus learning.

The problem

The "What do you want for dinner tonight?"
"Don't know, you?"
"I don't know either"
 conversation is something that comes up practically every day in our house. Also my partner and I are currently trying to eat healthier. So there's a need for meal planning.

There are of course already many resources out there to help with this. We've got a shelf full of recipe books in the kitchen. A few websites bookmarked, some subreddits I know of around meal planning, and of course, Google. Yet we're still pretty terrible at regularly finding culinary inspiration, and at buying ingredients that open up new options when we do the weekly shop.

Let's solve this problem.

But Graham, you might be wondering, why is this on your website about learning to code?

Well:

  • It'll give me one home to keep recipes and ideas that are currently spread out across various different homes
  • If it's on my own site that I maintain, I'll probably look at it regularly
  • I can use technology to get exactly what I want out of it.
    • And maybe most importantly:
  • I don't yet know what technology I could or should use to do it. So building it should be a great learning experience.

The spec

On the homepage of my site, whenever I load it, I want to see a recipe to inspire me. I also want an option to instead see a recipe for something I've made before and enjoyed, in case I'm not in the mood to try something

As a site user on the homepage:

  • On page load, I see a random recipe that has not yet been made
  • I can click a Random button to display another recipe, regardless if it's been made before
  • I can click a New button to display another random recipe that has not yet been made
  • I can click a Favourite button to display a recipe that has previously been made and rated as good.
  • I can click a View All button to be taken to the collection of recipes

As a site user maintaining the collection of recipes:

  • I can add details of a new recipe
    • This could be text, an image, or a link to external content
  • I can delete an existing recipe
  • I can edit an existing recipe

As a site user viewing a recipe:

  • I can mark a recipe as Made
  • I can mark a Made recipe as Good, would make again or Nah, didn't love it
  • I can edit the recipe
  • I can add a note to the recipe
